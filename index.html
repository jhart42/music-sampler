Netherfriends

A Performer You Won't Forget

Netherfriends is a one-man-band consisting of LA-based artist, Shawn Rosenblatt. This semi-crazed artist is currently on a mission to release three albums every month this year (2018). From stages across the U.S., Netherfriends is known for creating an electric environment when performing.

If there were every such a thing as DIY music artist, Netherfriends would be the standard. He writes, produces, and records his own music, in addition to hand-making his own merchandise. After his head-lining performance at the Swamp Records Closing Party in May 2018, he shocked the crowd by personally selling his merchandise afterwards- straight from the stage. Regardless of his barrier-breaking manor, he was genuinely fun to listen to and made the crowd come together for a great night.
